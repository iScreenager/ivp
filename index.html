About Indus Valley Partners



We are a niche Solutions and Services firm founded in 2000 and presently with 600+ full time employees globally, focused on servicing the needs of the alternative asset management space (i.e. Hedge Funds, Private Equity Funds and Fund of Funds). Established in 2000, IVP has built a unique business covering Services, Products and Managed Services around unique proprietary technology that it has developed inhouse over the last 15+ years focused on the asset management domain. IVP services more than 120 fund managers (hedge funds, private equity, credit, asset managers and pension funds) who manage more than 2.5 trillion dollars in assets using IVP technology today.

Culturally IVP is a young, vibrant and agile organization. With our average age group being between 27 and 28, IVP is bustling with activity, engagement and fun. As a well-being focused organization, we consider continuous engagement, transparent communication and merit-based opportunities to be pivotal to building and sustaining a healthy work environment. With a flattish hierarchy and lean project teams, IVP is proud of enabling an open-door culture that makes sharing of opinions, asking questions and accessing senior leaders, very easy.

For more information, visit www.indusvalleypartners.com.

About The Event



We at IVP are thrilled to announce the launch of Coderush, our online hackathon for 2023. We are constantly on the lookout for tech talent that can bring innovative ideas to the table, add value to our product suite and in the process build a great career for themselves at IVP.

Coderush brings to you an incredible opportunity to test your coding ability, solve some challenging coding questions and take away some exciting prizes. We will also be offering PPI to all the shortlisted candidates. After the grand finale within one week we will be scheduling the interviews for the shortlisted candidates (1 Winner, 1 Runner up and 13 Finalist).
